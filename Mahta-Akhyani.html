My Current Work

Social Robot for eliciting empathy to help children with ASD

Affective expression approach has attracted interest in robotics.The capability to perceive and express emotions is considered to be important for creating truly socially interactive robots. Many different types of robots have been used by various research groups to engage autistic children in playful interactions.

The system includes a robot, a tablet, and a jetson nano which acts like the "brain" of the robot. The tablet displays a web-based survey interface designed professionally illustrated videos that support the music that's playing, or lip syncing with the robot's speech.

This model is implemented into the system with Robotic Operating System (ROS) and it has a camera which allows the robot to see the face of the participant for imitating the expressions.
